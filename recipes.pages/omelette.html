Cheese Omelette

Description

An omelette never hits wrong!

This recipe ensures you get a creamy and fluffy omelette everytime.

Ingredients

  • 6 large eggs
  • 0.3 dl milk (or as needed)
  • Salt and freshly ground white pepper (to taste)
  • 55 grams butter
  • 1.2 dl shredded Emmentaler cheese (or cheese of choosing)
