Tuna Salad

Description

A fresh Tuna Salad, that is also a perfect post-workout meal.

Enjoy whith a fresh salad or toasted bread!

Ingredients

  • 70 grams tuna, drained (½ of a 5-ounce can is 2.5 oz)
  • 0.6 dl mayonnaise (¼ cup)
  • 0.3 dl chopped celery (⅛ cup)
  • 0.3 dl chopped onion (⅛ cup)
  • ½ tbs chopped fresh parsley (or 1.5 tsp / 0.07 dl)
  • ¼ tsp lemon juice
  • ⅛ tsp garlic powder
  • ¹⁄₁₆ tsp salt (a tiny pinch)
  • ¹⁄₁₆ tsp ground black pepper
  • ½ pinch paprika (to taste)

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients.
  2. Combine tuna, mayonnaise, celery, onion, parsley, lemon juice, garlic powder, salt, and pepper in a large bowl; mix well. Season with paprika; refrigerate until chilled.
