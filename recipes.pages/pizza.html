Pepperoni Pizza

Description

Pepperoni Pizza do not need an introduction.

This one has a thin crust and lots of enjoyement.

Ingredients

  • 0.6 dl water
  • 0.9 dl tomato paste
  • ½ tsp dried oregano, crushed
  • ½ tsp dried basil, crushed
  • ¼ tsp garlic powder
  • ¼ tsp onion powder
  • ¼ tsp sugar
  • ¼ tsp salt
  • ⅛ tsp black pepper
  • 1.2 dl shredded mozzarella cheese (½ cup)
  • 85 grams pepperoni (½ of a 6-ounce package is 3 oz)
